Who Are We?

Welcome to the Robotics Club of Central Florida.

This is an entirely student run organization. We focus on creating innovative and competitive robots.

Every year, we take on projects that involve demonstrations of robotics, pushing the bounds of automation, and innovative, robust designs. We are always looking for members join in the advancement of the demanding robotics field.

Currently, we are realizing three challenging projects...

Bowser

Our autonomous robot.

Bowser uses computer vision to avoid objects.

Citrobot

Our combat robot.

Citrobot is a 15lb combat robot designed entirely in house.

Demobot

Our demonstration robot.

Demobot demonstrates all aspects of robotics.
